# Get In Touch With Me

I cover cybersecurity, national security, technology, and data for [Mother Jones](). If you have something you think I should take a look at please don't hesitate to let me know. Each method of contact carries its own complications and level of anonymity. Please consider your risk level when reaching out. For instance, if sending sensitive materials your employer would not approve you sending, please don't use your work computer or phone. 

*The approach to how I structured this page was inspired by the* LA Times' *[news tips](https://www.latimes.com/tips/) page, the* [ProPublica](https://www.propublica.org/leak-to-us/) *news tips page, and pages created by individual journalists such as TK and TK.*
